## How do I reset my password?

You receive an email with a secure reset link when you initiate the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Login" icon="login">
    Visit the Pare Teknoloji login page at `https://docs.pareteknoloji.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password" icon="key">
    Enter your email address and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Email Link" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox and click the reset link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in your account settings for added security.
</Callout>

## Can I invite team members?

Yes, you manage teams from the dashboard.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Adding a New Team Member" default-open="true">
    1. Go to **Teams** > **Members**.
    2. Click **Invite**.
    3. Enter their email and select role (`admin` or `editor`).
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Role Permissions">
    - `admin`: Full access, including billing.
    - `editor`: Edit docs, no deletes.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Why does my page fail to parse?

MDX parsing errors often stem from unescaped special characters like `{` or `<`.

<Callout kind="alert" show-icon="true">
  Always wrap code-like text in backticks: `{API_KEY}` or `<Component>`.
</Callout>

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,YAML">
  ```javascript
  // Wrong - causes parse error
  const config = { key: "value" };

  // Correct - use valid syntax
  const config = { key: "value" };
  ```
  ```yaml
  # Wrong frontmatter
  ---
  title: Page {with braces}
  ---

  # Correct
  ---
  title: "Page Title"
  ---
  ```
</CodeGroup>

## Page not updating after edits?

Clear your browser cache or check the preview mode.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Force Refresh" icon="refresh-cw">
    Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (`Cmd+Shift+R` on macOS).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Preview" icon="eye">
    Toggle between **Edit** and **Preview** modes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Changes" icon="upload">
    Click **Publish** to deploy live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How do I structure MDX files effectively?

Start every file with valid YAML frontmatter, then use H2 headings.

```yaml
---
title: "Your Page Title"
description: "Brief summary."
---
```
